Martin Goodman

Martin Goodman [1908-1992] was an American publisher of pulp magazines, paperback books, men's adventure magazines, and comic books, launching the company that would become Marvel Comics.

Stan Lee

Stanley Martin Lieber [1922-2018] was an American comic book writer, editor, publisher and producer.He rose through the ranks of a family-run business called Timely Comics which would later become Marvel Comics.

Robert Downey Jr.

Robert John Downey Jr. [1965] is an American actor and producer. Downey gained global recognition for starring as Tony Stark/Iron Man in ten films, beginning with Iron Man (2008), and leading up to Avengers:Endgame (2019).
